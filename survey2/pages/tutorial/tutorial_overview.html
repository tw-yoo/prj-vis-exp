Tutorial (2/3)

Tutorial Overview

This page simulates one round of the survey. Please read the content carefully and follow the instructions. Your responses on this tutorial page will not be recorded or saved.

In this tutorial, you'll get familiar with what you'll see in the actual survey. The screen is divided into three main sections:

  • Left Panel: A chart, a related question about the chart, and an answer to that question. This panel is scrollable.
  • Right Panel: A step-by-step explanation showing why the provided answer is correct. This panel is scrollable.
  • Questions Section: A list of questions you will answer in this round. This section appears below the panels.

During the survey, please follow these steps carefully:

  1. Start with the left panel: Study the chart thoroughly, read the question carefully, and check the provided answer.
  2. Next, move to the right panel: Read the detailed explanation provided, step-by-step, to see how the answer was derived.
  3. Evaluate the answer and explanation: Consider whether the provided answer and its explanation clearly match your own interpretation of the chart.
  4. Respond below: After reviewing everything, answer two questions at the bottom of the page to indicate whether the answer is correct and how clear both the answer and the explanation are.

Note: There are two questions (Q1 and Q2). The time from when you enter the page until you answer Q1 will be recorded. The time spent on Q2 will not be recorded. Please answer Q1 first before proceeding to Q2.

On the next page, you'll see the actual survey screen. When you're ready, click Next to continue.
