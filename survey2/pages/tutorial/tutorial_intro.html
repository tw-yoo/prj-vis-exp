Tutorial

Welcome to the tutorial for our chart-based explanation study. This tutorial will guide you through what you will see and how to interact with each part of the survey.

In each round, you will encounter:

  • Chart: A bar chart or a line chart displaying data.
  • Question: A question asking you to interpret the chart.
  • Answer: The correct answer to the question.
  • Explanation: A detailed explanation of why the answer is correct, which may include text and visual elements.

We will record:

  • Time Spent: How long you take to read the explanation and submit your answer.
  • Accuracy: Whether your answer matches the correct answer. Accuracy is most important, so please answer based on your intuitive understanding.

This tutorial consists of 2 practice rounds. You can navigate using the Previous and Next buttons, and your responses will be saved if you refresh or navigate back.

Once you feel comfortable, click Next to begin the practice rounds.
