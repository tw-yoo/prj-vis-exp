Tutorial (1/3)

Welcome to the tutorial for our chart-based explanation study. This tutorial will guide you through what you will see and how to interact with each part of the survey.

Survey Procedure Overview

The survey will be conducted as follows:

  1. Four explanation methods will be randomly assigned to you in a unique order.
  2. For each explanation method, you will complete 5 rounds. In each round, you will review a chart, question, provided answer, and explanation, then complete a short evaluation task.
  3. After completing all 5 rounds of each explanation method, you'll answer a brief questionnaire (both multiple-choice and open-ended) about your experience with that method. This step will repeat 4 times, once for each explanation method.
  4. Once you finish evaluating all four explanation methods, you will complete a final questionnaire comparing and ranking your preferences among the methods. This questionnaire will also include both multiple-choice and open-ended questions.
  5. After finishing the final survey, you will receive a completion code. Please send this code via email to twyoo@yonsei.ac.kr to receive your participation reward.

This tutorial consists of 2 practice rounds. You can navigate using the Previous and Next buttons, and your responses will be saved if you refresh or navigate back.

Once you feel comfortable, click Next to begin the practice rounds.
