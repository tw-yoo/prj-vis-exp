Thank you for your interest in our research!

This study investigates how different explanation styles for chart-based questions affect users’ understanding. You will be shown four distinct explanation methods (referred to as “Method 1” through “Method 4”), either in a fixed order or in a random order. After reading each explanation, please answer the questions and provide your feedback.

Survey Procedure: (1) Tutorial → (2) View chart and explanations, complete the survey → (3) Survey completion (receive a completion code)

We will record:

  • Time Spent: How long you take to read the explanation and submit your answer.
  • Accuracy: Whether your answer matches the correct answer. Accuracy is most important, so please answer based on your intuitive understanding.

Instructions for Completing the Survey

  • Please complete the survey in one sitting. If you leave the page or refresh, your responses will not be saved. Please ensure you have enough time to finish the survey in one go.
  • Participants who complete the entire survey will receive a gift card worth ₩30,000. If you do not finish the survey, you will not be eligible for the reward.
  • If you encounter any confusion or difficulties during the survey, please describe them in detail in the open-ended questions. We appreciate your feedback.
