Main Survey

Now that you've completed the tutorial, you'll begin the actual survey. Please keep the following in mind:

  • Each round will present a chart, a related question, the provided answer, and an explanation—just like in the tutorial.
  • After reviewing each round, evaluate the accuracy of the answer and explanation using the provided rating scales.
  • You will complete 4 explanation methods, each consisting of 5 rounds, followed by brief feedback questionnaires.
  • Your responses are recorded for the study and cannot be changed once submitted.
  • Your progress is saved automatically. Use the Previous and Next buttons to navigate.

Instructions for Completing the Survey

  • Please complete the survey in one sitting. If you leave the page or refresh, your responses will not be saved. Please ensure you have enough time to finish the survey in one go.
  • Participants who complete the entire survey will receive a gift card worth ₩30,000. If you do not finish the survey, you will not be eligible for the reward.
  • If you encounter any confusion or difficulties during the survey, please describe them in detail in the open-ended questions. We appreciate your feedback.
