Thank you for your interest in our research!

This study investigates how different system-generated explanations for chart-based questions affect users’ understanding. You will be shown four distinct outputs generated by our system (labeled “Output 1” through “Output 4”) in a random order. After reviewing each output, please answer the accompanying questions and provide your feedback.

Survey Procedure: (1) Tutorial → (2) Main Survey → (3) Completion Code & Reward

The main survey will be conducted as follows:

  1. Four types of system-generated explanations will be randomly assigned to you in a unique order. Here, an “explanation” refers to the reasoning process showing how the provided answer to a chart-based question was derived.
  2. For each type of system-generated output, you will complete 5 rounds. In each round, you will review a chart, a question, the provided answer, and the system’s output, then complete a short evaluation task.
  3. After completing all 5 rounds for a type of system-generated output, you'll answer a brief questionnaire about your experience with that output. This step will repeat 4 times, once for each type of system-generated output.
  4. After you have evaluated all four types of system-generated output, you will complete a final questionnaire. In this questionnaire, you will compare and rank your preferences among the outputs, and provide any additional feedback.
  5. After finishing the final survey, you will receive a completion code along with the contact email address. Please send this code to that email to receive your participation reward.

We will record:

  • Time Spent: How long you take to read the system’s output and submit your answers.
  • Your Responses: Your judgments and feedback about the system-generated output.
  • Accuracy: Whether your answer matches the correct answer. Accuracy matters most, so please answer based on your best understanding.
