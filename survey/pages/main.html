Thank you for your interest in our research!

This study investigates how different system-generated explanations for chart-based questions affect users’ comprehension and reasoning about chart. You will be shown three distinct outputs generated by our system (labeled “Output 1” through “Output 3”) in a random order. After reviewing each output, please answer the accompanying questions and provide your feedback.

Survey Procedure: (1) Tutorial → (2) Main Survey → (3) Post-Study Survey

The main survey will be conducted as follows:

  1. You will complete 20 rounds. In each round, you will review a chart, a question, the provided answer, and the system-generated explanation, and then complete a short evaluation.
  2. After finishing the 20 rounds, you will answer a brief questionnaire about your overall experience with the explanations.
  3. At the very end, you will complete a final questionnaire where you can compare your preferences and provide additional feedback.

We will record:

  • Time Spent: How long you take to read the system’s output and submit your answers.
  • Your Responses: Your judgments and feedback about the system-generated output.
  • Accuracy: Whether your answer matches the correct answer. Accuracy matters most, so please answer based on your best understanding.

Instructions for Completing the Survey

  • Please complete the survey in one sitting. It will take about 30 minutes to complete. We record the total time you spend on the survey. If you leave the page or refresh it, your responses will not be saved. Make sure you have enough time to finish the entire survey in one session.
  • During the study, please make your best decision.
  • Participants who complete the entire survey will receive a gift card worth ₩30,000. At the end of the survey, we will provide you with a six-character alphanumeric code along with the contact email address. Please send the code to that email to claim your reward. If you do not complete the survey, you will not be eligible for the reward.
