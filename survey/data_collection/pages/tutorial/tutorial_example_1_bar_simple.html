Example Question & Answer

This is a pre-filled example. Study how the question is structured.

1. Question:
What is the average market share of the top 3 companies?
2. Answer:
0.126
3. Operations Used:
Sort Nth Average
4. Explanation (Step-by-step):
1. Sort companies by market share in descending order 2. Select the top 3 companies: Conad (0.148), Coop Italia (0.129), Selex (0.101) 3. Calculate the average: (0.148 + 0.129 + 0.101) / 3 = 0.126
