Example Question & Answer

This example shows filtering by Y-axis values.

1. Question:
How many years have enterprise numbers above the overall average?
2. Answer:
4
3. Operations Used:
Average Filter Count
4. Explanation (Step-by-step):
1. Calculate the overall average across all years - Average = 498722.56 2. Filter to find years where number of enterprises > 498722.56 - Filtered years: 2012, 2013, 2014, 2016 3. Count the number of filtered years: 4
